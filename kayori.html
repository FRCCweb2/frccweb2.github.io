A tale of friendship and endurance

Kayori is a young boy living in the mighty Amazon rainforest.


When a deadly disease strikes his tribe and he is the only one still healthy, he must leave his home and journey to a distant mountain. A powerful goddess dwells at the summit in the form of a feathered serpent, and it is rumored that just one of her feathers can cure any disease.


Along the way Kayori will make unexpected friendships and overcome insurmountable odds.
