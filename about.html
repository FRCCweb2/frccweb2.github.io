Sarah Hickman is an author and illustrator of middle-grade graphic novels. Her titles are Kayori, The Twilight Reaper, and Atka-The Greatest Hunter. She is currently working on a new science fiction story, Barnard's Star, which follows a young musician trying to find her place in the galaxy. Check out her newsletter for more information and updates.


In her spare time, Sarah loves to hike the beautiful Colorado mountains, follow her favorite bands on tour, and play around on the guitar.
