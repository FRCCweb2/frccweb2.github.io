Will Atka learn what it truly means to be a hunter?

Atka is determined to prove that she is ready to be a hunter.


However, she still has not learned to respect the spirits of the animals that she takes, often forgetting to thank them. Her father fears that this will anger the spirits and bring misfortune on the tribe.


Everything changes for Atka when she loses her life trying to hunt on her own. Now she must journey through the spirit world, a place full of the twisted souls of animals that were not respected and are ready to devour the unwary.
